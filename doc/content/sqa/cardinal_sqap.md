## [!ac](SQAP) id=software_quality_assurance_plan

The Cardinal [!ac](SQAP) is titled "Software Quality Assurance Plan: Cardinal" (ANL/NSE-25/65) and is adapted from the MOOSE [!ac](SQAP).
This plan lays out the specifics of how Cardinal ensures that life-cycle activities are planned and performed in a traceable and orderly manner.  
It includes the requirements for [!ac](CMP), [!ac](VVP), and [!ac](SQP).
The most recent version of the [!ac](SQAP) is available for download from [OSTI](https://www.osti.gov/biblio/3001333).
